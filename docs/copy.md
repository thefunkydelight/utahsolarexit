**Q: How long does the cancellation process take?**
A: Please note that case times may vary. However, you will remain in constant contact with the law firm throughout your contract cancellation.

**Q: What types of solar contracts can you help with?**
A: We help with solar leases, PPAs (Power Purchase Agreements), loan contracts, and owned-system agreements. If you're in Utah and feel trapped, contact us for a free review.

### Our Process
**Q: What happens during the free consultation?**
A: We learn about your situation, review your contract type, and answer your initial questions — no obligation.

**Q: Will canceling hurt my credit?**
A: It depends on your contract and situation. The professionals we connect you with work to minimize risks and protect your financial standing throughout the process.

### Costs & Fees
**Q: How much does your service cost?**
A: Your initial consultation is completely free. We will assess your situation, explain your options, and provide transparent pricing before you commit to anything.

### Consumer Rights
