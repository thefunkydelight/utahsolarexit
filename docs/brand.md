# Brand & Design System

## Business Identity
- **Brand Name:** Solar Exit Utah
- **Tagline:** Expert Guidance Out of Unfair Solar Agreements
- **Market:** Utah homeowners trapped in unfair solar contracts

## Color Palette

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Background (primary) | `#0A0A0A` | Page background, nav |
| Background (card) | `#1A1A19` | Cards, sections |
| Background (subtle) | `#34322D` | Borders, dividers |
| Text (primary) | `#F8F8F7` | Headings, body |
| Text (muted) | `#858481` | Secondary text, captions |
| Accent (gold) | `#FFD700` | CTAs, icons, highlights |
| Accent (gold hover) | `#F0A500` | Hover states |
| Overlay | `#0A0A0A` at 90% opacity | Hero overlay |

## Typography

| Font | Weights | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Space Grotesk | 400, 500, 600, 700, 800 | Headings, CTAs, nav |
| Inter | 400, 500, 600, 700 | Body copy, form labels |

## Visual Style
- **Theme:** Dark, premium, trustworthy
- **Aesthetic:** High-contrast dark backgrounds with gold accents
- **Icons:** Outline SVG style, gold (`#FFD700`) stroke
- **Images:** Full-bleed hero with dark overlay; house/solar imagery
- **Borders:** Subtle `#34322D` or low-opacity white
- **Border radius:** Rounded corners (approx `rounded-xl` / 12px on cards)
- **Shadows:** Minimal, dark-on-dark

## Spacing & Layout
- Max content width: ~1280px centered
- Section padding: 80px–120px vertical
- Card padding: 32px
- Hero: Full viewport height, centered text

## Badges / Trust Signals
- BBB Accredited badge
- Google Reviews badge
- Trustpilot badge
